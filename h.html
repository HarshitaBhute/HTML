01 · Overview
Problem & Approach

HSR Motors relies on a shared spreadsheet to manage hundreds of leads flowing in from Facebook, Google, Twitter, walk-ins, and website forms. This creates data silos, no real-time visibility, zero automation, and massive manual overhead. LeadPilot is a browser-based CRM tailored to the dealership's exact workflow — giving the sales team instant access to prioritised lead queues and giving the business manager a live analytics cockpit.

🎯 Core Problem

Spreadsheets are single-user, static, and error-prone. Leads fall through the cracks. There is no audit trail, no assignment mechanism, and no way to measure funnel health in real time.

💡 Our Solution

LeadPilot centralises every lead in one place with role-based views. Sales reps see their prioritised call queue; managers see live dashboards. Automation handles routine tasks so people can focus on selling.

👤 User 1 — Sales Team

Needs a fast, distraction-free list of leads to call, with quick-update status controls and a call-log history so no context is ever lost between shifts.

📊 User 2 — Business Manager

Needs a one-glance dashboard showing total leads, source breakdown, funnel conversion rates, and team performance — all without downloading any exports.

02 · Features
What Makes LeadPilot Stand Out
F-01

Smart Lead Inbox

Leads auto-sorted by source, recency, and priority score. A single-click call initiator with a built-in call timer so reps never forget to log duration.

F-02

Kanban Pipeline Board

Drag-and-drop cards across New → Contacted → Interested → Qualified → Closed/Lost. Status updates reflect instantly for all team members.

F-03

AI Priority Scoring

Each lead is automatically scored 1–10 based on source, response speed, and vehicle interest. Hot leads surface at the top of the queue automatically.

F-04

Auto-Assignment Engine

Round-robin assignment ensures every new lead is immediately assigned to an available sales rep — no lead sits unowned for more than 5 minutes.

F-05

Call & Note Logger

Every interaction is timestamped and saved automatically. Reps can add quick voice-to-text notes after each call without disrupting their workflow.

F-06

Live Manager Dashboard

Business managers see real-time funnel metrics, source ROI, team leaderboard, and daily/weekly trend graphs — no refresh, no export needed.

F-07

Follow-up Reminders

Reps can schedule follow-ups directly on a lead card. LeadPilot sends in-app and SMS nudges so no callback is ever missed.

F-08

Multi-Source Integration

Leads from Facebook Lead Ads, Google Ads, Twitter, website forms, and manual walk-in entry all flow into a single unified feed automatically.

F-09

Bulk Actions & Exports

Managers can select, reassign, or export batches of leads. CSV exports with date-range filters for monthly reporting in seconds.
